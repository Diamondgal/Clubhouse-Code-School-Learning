Cancer Association of South Africa

The work:
When my friend's cancer reappeared, I promised her that she wouldn't be alone during her treatment. She told me that she couldn't face loosing her hair again and the public scrutiny of such a privately painful journey that made her feel so different from everyone else. So I told her I would get the whole of South Africa to shave their heads. (or spray them a wild color if they would not shave). And so began my journey into cause-related marketing.(Emily Dickinson's words could not have a stronger resounding alarm bell here : a word once said is dead some say, others say, they only begin to live that day’).
My Effort:
With another special friend and business partner, I initiated and delivered the National CANSA-SHAVATHON across South Africa for 3 years - raising over R25 million rand, achieving a Guinness World Record for all the South African’s who shaved or sprayed, shaving my own head bald and encouraging people to show their hearts by supporting the national event at multiple locations. (but most importantly - I continue to enjoy the friendship of my surviving friend). Shavathon remains the Cancer Association of South Africa's single biggest fund-raiser and a national iconic brand. Heart work as well as pioneering in the field of cause-related marketing.
